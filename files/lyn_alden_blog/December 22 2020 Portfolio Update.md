# Portfolio Update: TSLA and GBTC

*December 22, 2020*

![Premium Feature Image](https://www.lynalden.com/wp-content/uploads/premium-feature.jpg)

This is a quick note to update readers about actions discussed in the recent December 20th report.

I had a big section in the Dec 20th report about a potential setup for a Tesla (TSLA) short. Here was the main action item:

> If Tesla remains below $700 next week, I think a small short position to hedge an otherwise long equity portfolio is worthwhile, with a cover point at $700 if Tesla goes higher than that.
>
> -Lyn Alden, December 20th

I went ahead and shorted it on Monday at around $660/share with a cover point of $695, and updated the members area in real time. It can also be seen in the Other Holdings tab of the model portfolio file in my Google Drive. This can serve as a bit of a portfolio hedge for a mostly long equity portfolio, but requires risk management, since shorting popular stocks is risky.

In the Dec 20th report, I also talked about potentially trimming the Grayscale Bitcoin Trust (GBTC) from the No Limits portfolio:

> I may trim GBTC in the No Limits portfolio a bit if it becomes too large of a position. It began as a 5% position, and is now more than a 10% position due to appreciation, despite no additional purchases. GBTC has a 30% premium over NAV, so it’s a less pure vehicle than directly owning Bitcoin itself (which I do for my actual core position), and thus if the premium gets too big, I may trim a bit in the coming months. However, I’m not quite at that point yet.
>
> -Lyn Alden, December 20th

Today I trimmed 100 shares of GBTC, which was 20% of the GBTC position. The premium vs NAV is now 40%, and the position became the largest in the portfolio at over 12%, so this was mainly for risk management. The shares tripled in price since the purchase point this summer, so a portion of the gains are now booked. This was also updated in the members area in real time.

![GBTC Premium](https://www.lynalden.com/wp-content/uploads/premium-2020-12-22-gbtc-premium.png)

I’m not changing my cold storage personal Bitcoin position; just trimming GBTC in this particular portfolio.

I’m publishing this note and sending out an email to ensure folks are aware of some of these actions, since they were discussed as potentials in the Dec 20th report and updated in the members area, but not everyone gets a chance to check the members area regularly. This is not investment advice; just what I’m doing with a couple of my positions.
