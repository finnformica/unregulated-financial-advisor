# Portfolio Update

*April 5, 2021*

![Premium Feature Image](https://www.lynalden.com/wp-content/uploads/premium-feature.jpg)

This is a brief note between normal premium reports. The next normal premium report is scheduled for next week as usual for its twice-monthly schedule.

The main reason for this note is that I just published my April 2021 public newsletter, and wanted to make sure premium members see it as well:

* [April 2021 Newsletter: Seeking Equilibrium](https://www.lynalden.com/april-2021-newsletter/)

In addition, I plan to make a minor portfolio change this week:

**Newsletter Portfolio**

-Sell Nucor (NUE) in the dividend section. I added this one on March 19th, 2020 at $30/share. A little over a year later, it’s $80/share, and paid dividends along the way. However, after such a strong run, I have a lot less visibility going forward about its prospects, and see more compelling opportunities elsewhere.

-Buy Cigna (CI) as a 3% position to replace Nucor. Cigna is an insurance company and pharmacy benefits manager.

![CI FASTGraph](https://www.lynalden.com/wp-content/uploads/premium-2021-4-5-ci-fastgraph.png)

*Chart Source: F.A.S.T. Graphs*

Cigna had a nice run lately, but mainly just broke out of a 3-year sideways consolidation. It remains at historically average valuations, and attractive absolute valuations. I like it as a value stock at the current time, considering risks and possible rewards.
